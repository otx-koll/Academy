데이터베이스 jspdb를 사용ㅇ하는 게정설정

  
  GRANT ALL PRIVILEGES
  ON jspdb.* TO 'myid'@'localhost'
  IDENTIFIED BY 'mypwd';


  GRANT ALL PRIVILEGES
  ON jspdb.* TO 'myid'@'%'
  IDENTIFIED BY 'mypwd';
